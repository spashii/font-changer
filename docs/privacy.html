Font Changer

Privacy Policy

Last updated 13 June 2026

Font Changer is built to need no information about you.

  • No tracking, no analytics, no advertising, no accounts.
  • No data is collected, transmitted, or sold. Nothing leaves your device.
  • Your settings (fonts, sizes, spacing, and per-site customizations) are stored locally on your device using the browser's extension storage.
  • All fonts are bundled inside the app. The extension never loads fonts or any other resource from the internet.
  • The extension only restyles the pages you visit. It does not read, record, or transmit your browsing history or page contents.

The website-access permission that Safari asks for is required only so the extension can apply your chosen font to the pages you view. It is used for nothing else.
